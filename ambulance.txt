The title of my project is Ambulance Detector.The main concept behind the project is to provide a smooth flow for the ambulance to reach the hospitals in time and there by minimizing the delay caused by traffic congestion.


the technologies used are Iot,Hardware used is ardinuo,RFID receiver and transmitter.

The  RFID system is used to alter the traffic lights upon its arrival at traffic light junction which would save lives at critical time.
